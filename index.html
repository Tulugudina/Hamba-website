About Hamba

"Hamba" means saving resources for future generations. Hamba PLC is an agroforestry company based in Ethiopia focused on planting trees, producing honey, spices, coffee and regenerating landscapes using climate-smart practices.

  • Large-scale tree planting & forest management
  • Spice production (ginger, turmeric, cardamom)
  • Honey, coffee and fruit production
  • Seedling production and consultancy
